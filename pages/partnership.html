Partnership

Right partners have an incredible impact on your business success.

APN Advanced Consulting Partner

Promact is officially recognized by AWS as a vendor of Amazon services focused on strategic/IT consulting, cloud migration, and application development to various industries. We have been delivering AWS-based solutions since 2008 And now, as an APN Consulting Partner, we are even more efficient in helping businesses of different sizes architect, build, manage, migrate and scale AWS applications and solutions.

UiPath Silver Partner

Promact joined the UiPath Business Partner Program as a Silver Business Partner. Based on our track record of completed RPA projects, own RPA Center of Excellence and UiPath certified specialists on board, this status proves our ability to deliver enterprise-level RPA services and solutions.

Salesforce Partner

Promact has become a Salesforce Registered Partner confirming the company’s experience in Salesforce functionality implementation and custom workflows development, as well as Salesforce integration with other software solutions. Delivering Salesforce solutions for more than a decade, Promact has developed and enhanced Salesforce-based solutions for real estate, finance, telecom, and other industries.
